Our Mission

We believe that learning should be interactive, engaging, and accessible to everyone. Crux bridges the gap between traditional education and modern technology, providing students with tools they need to succeed in today's digital world. Our platform emphasizes hands-on learning, critical thinking, and practical application of knowledge.

Built with Modern Technology

Crux is built using cutting-edge web technologies to ensure a fast, reliable, and secure learning experience. Our platform features a responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices, from desktop computers to mobile phones.

Frontend: Angular, TypeScript, HTML5, CSS3
Backend: ASP .NET, Gemini and Google APIs, AWS Databases
Features: Real-time tracking, Interactive lessons, Personalized expirience
